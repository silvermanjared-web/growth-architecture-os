# Funnel Definitions

## Purpose

Use shared language for the steps between first interest and final outcome.

## Common stages

### Visit

A person reaches a digital experience.

### Inquiry

A person expresses interest.

### Qualified inquiry

The inquiry contains enough fit or intent to deserve follow-up.

### Connect

The person reaches a meaningful human or system touchpoint.

### Application or opportunity

The person enters a deeper evaluation or buying path.

### Final outcome

The business result the system is built to produce.

## Rule

Do not compare funnel stages unless the definitions are consistent.
