# KPI Dictionary

## Purpose

Define the core measures used in growth conversations.

## Principles

- Use plain language.
- Define each measure before using it in a summary.
- Separate early activity from final business value.
- Keep the business outcome visible.

## Common measures

### Volume

How much activity the system produced.

### Cost

What it took to produce that activity.

### Quality

Whether the activity moved toward the desired outcome.

### Progression

How many people or accounts moved to the next meaningful step.

### Final outcome

The result the business actually cares about.
