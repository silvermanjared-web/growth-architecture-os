# Taxonomy and Reporting Standards

## Purpose

A growth system needs names that people can understand and systems can organize.

## Naming principles

- Keep names readable.
- Keep the same order across programs.
- Avoid unnecessary abbreviations.
- Use dates only when they clarify timing.
- Make ownership clear where possible.

## Reporting principles

- Start with the business question.
- Separate activity from progress.
- Note uncertainty clearly.
- Keep recommendations tied to the evidence.
- Make next steps visible.

## Standard

The goal is not more reporting. The goal is cleaner decisions.
