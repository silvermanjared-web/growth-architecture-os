## Claim discipline

Do not overstate. Do not strip useful hedges like `roughly`, `associated with`, `source materials cite`, or `informing planning` when the source uses that level of caution.

The power is in the pattern: complex environments, operating-model clarity, measurable outcomes, and stronger decision infrastructure.
