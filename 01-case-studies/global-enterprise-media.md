# Case Study: Enterprise Media Standards

## Context

Large media programs need shared standards across teams and regions.

## Challenge

The work required consistency without removing local judgment.

## Approach

Jared supported:

- planning structure
- shared standards
- team enablement
- practical documentation
- leadership visibility

## Outcome

The work helped make planning more consistent and easier to activate across complex teams.

## Reusable lesson

Scale depends on shared language, clear routines, and enough flexibility for local context.
