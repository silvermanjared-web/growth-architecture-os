# Case Study: Rapid Campaign Launch System

## Context

A multi-brand education environment needed fast campaign restructuring across state-level programs during a seasonal enrollment window.

## Challenge

The work required speed without losing QA, pacing discipline, or cross-team visibility.

## Approach

Built a scalable launch workflow across campaign build, QA, pacing, creative refresh, and go-to-market testing.

## Outcome

Public portfolio materials cite 60+ campaigns relaunched and restructured in under one month, with improved cross-team visibility, speed, and campaign agility.

## Jared's role

Led launch strategy, built QA systems, and collaborated across analytics, media, and stakeholder groups to hit aggressive timing.

## Reusable lesson

Speed only works when the operating system is clear. The faster the launch, the more important the QA and pacing model become.
