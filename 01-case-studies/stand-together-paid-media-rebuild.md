# Case Study: Paid Media Operating Rebuild

## Context

A large mission-driven organization needed a stronger paid media operating model across channels, partners, and reporting routines.

## Challenge

The work required more than campaign execution. The system needed clearer planning, cleaner communication, stronger partner accountability, and a better way to connect activity to quality engagement.

## Approach

Jared helped rebuild the operating model around:

- clearer channel roles
- partner routines
- quality-focused readouts
- team operating rhythm
- practical decision support

## Outcome

Source materials cite strong year-over-year growth in web-qualified engagement and performance above benchmark expectations.

## Reusable lesson

Paid media becomes more valuable when it is managed as an operating system, not a collection of platform tasks.
