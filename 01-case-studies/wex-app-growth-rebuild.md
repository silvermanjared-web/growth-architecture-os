# Case Study: App Growth Rebuild

## Context

A financial services app needed stronger acquisition performance and a cleaner operating model across paid search, quality assurance, and measurement.

## Challenge

The acquisition program was underperforming because account structure, KPI alignment, and QA routines were not strong enough for the growth stage.

## Approach

Led a full-funnel rebuild:

- Reworked account structure
- Added stronger QA routines
- Improved pacing discipline
- Systematized app-led measurement
- Aligned media, analytics, product, and creative inputs

## Outcome

Public portfolio materials cite a 500% lift in approved applications and an internal benchmark for app-led growth.

## Reusable lesson

Performance improves when the account rebuild is paired with operating discipline. Structure, signal quality, and QA matter as much as bidding tactics.
