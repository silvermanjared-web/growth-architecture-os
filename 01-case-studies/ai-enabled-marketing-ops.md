# Case Study: AI-Enabled Marketing Operations

## Context

Growth teams often repeat the same diagnostic, reporting, and communication work every week.

## Challenge

The opportunity was to make repeated work faster without lowering judgment or creating generic output.

## Approach

Jared built AI-assisted workflows around:

- performance summaries
- executive briefs
- case study extraction
- communication QA
- playbook creation
- source-aware drafting

## Outcome

The work created a practical operating layer for turning repeated marketing tasks into reusable systems.

## Reusable lesson

AI is most useful when it improves workflow quality and preserves human judgment.
