# Case Study: Global SEM Playbooks

## Context

A global B2B SaaS environment needed better alignment across regions, teams, and search strategy.

## Challenge

Regional agency teams operated with inconsistent SEM strategies, disconnected KPIs, and limited shared infrastructure.

## Approach

Created and deployed unified SEM playbooks across global regions. Supported in-market alignment, training, reporting standards, and shared planning routines.

## Outcome

Public portfolio materials cite 30%+ year-over-year revenue growth and adoption of unified SEM systems across business units.

## Jared's role

Led global SEM strategy and regional enablement, authored playbooks, and acted as connective tissue between regional teams and executive stakeholders.

## Reusable lesson

Global scale requires shared operating language: clear standards, local judgment, and repeatable playbooks.
