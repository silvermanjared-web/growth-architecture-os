# Case Study: FFIA Measurement Model

## Context

A mission-driven organization needed stronger trust in paid media reporting and clearer links between creative, media, and quality engagement.

## Challenge

Reporting was fragmented. Quality signals were not aligned clearly enough with business goals. Creative cycles needed a stronger connection to meaningful engagement.

## Approach

Built a reporting model around four practical moves:

- Fact finding
- Insight development
- Action planning
- Operating follow-through

The goal was to make performance conversations more useful: less reporting theater, more decision support.

## Outcome

The work supported 86% year-over-year growth and a 3x improvement in quality engagement, according to public portfolio materials.

## Jared's role

Authored the measurement model, led media and measurement strategy, and aligned creative and analytics work around a clearer performance standard.

## Reusable lesson

Trust improves when reporting moves from channel recap to decision logic.
