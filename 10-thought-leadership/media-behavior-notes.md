# Media Behavior Notes

## Core idea

Media strategy should follow how attention and trust actually move.

## Jared's credible lanes

- Creator-led distribution
- OTT and CTV planning
- Video-first social behavior
- Cross-channel media strategy
- Measurement clarity in newer channels

## Practical standard

Do not chase every platform trend. Define the role, clarify the signal, and connect the channel to a business decision.
