# Recruiter Talking Points

## One-line frame

I stabilize and scale growth systems.

## Stronger positioning

I am a growth and performance marketing leader who builds the operating layer behind scalable demand: measurement, governance, media allocation, CRO, agency accountability, and executive decision support.

## What I am best hired to fix

- Growth systems that have become too fragmented
- Media programs where spend is moving faster than visibility
- Agency models without enough accountability
- CRO programs that need structure and cadence
- Reporting that describes activity but does not support decisions
- AI workflows that need adoption, not theater

## What makes me different

I can move between systems and people without dropping the thread. I understand the media, the data, the creative, the politics, and the operating model.

## Questions to ask recruiters

- What problem is this role really being hired to solve?
- Is the need more tactical execution, operating-model design, or executive decision support?
- How much authority does the role have over measurement, budget, partners, and process?
- What is broken today that leadership wants fixed in the first 90 days?
