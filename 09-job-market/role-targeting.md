# Role Targeting

## Best-fit roles

- VP Growth
- VP Performance Marketing
- Head of Growth
- Head of Performance Marketing
- VP Marketing Operations
- Senior Director or VP Growth Strategy
- Chief of Staff to CMO in growth-heavy environments
- Marketing transformation leader
- AI-enabled marketing operations leader

## Best-fit environments

- PE-backed companies with complex growth systems
- Multi-brand portfolios
- Education, SaaS, healthcare, fintech, marketplace, advocacy, or enterprise services
- Companies moving from agency dependence to internal capability
- Companies with unclear measurement, budget, or channel governance
- Companies where growth has become too complex for the current operating model

## Strongest value proposition

I bring structure to messy growth environments.

That means clearer ownership, better signal quality, stronger media allocation logic, cleaner agency accountability, and executive-ready decision support.

## Less ideal fits

- Pure channel execution roles
- Roles with no authority over measurement, budget, or operating model
- Teams looking only for a tactical media buyer

## Interview positioning

The strongest frame is not: I run paid media.

The stronger frame is: I build the operating model that makes paid media, CRO, analytics, agencies, and leadership decisions work together.
