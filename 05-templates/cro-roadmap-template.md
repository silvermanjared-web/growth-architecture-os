# CRO Roadmap Template

## Opening line

Write one sentence that names the real conversion problem.

## Situation

Describe the current paths, audiences, and funnel structure.

## Diagnosis

Name the friction, proof gaps, measurement gaps, and ownership gaps.

## Thesis

State the operating principle behind the roadmap.

Example: optimize the business outcome, not the shallow conversion.

## Roadmap

List each workstream with location, impact, effort, phase, and owner.

## Phase plan

### Phase 0: foundation

Tracking, taxonomy, baseline, and QA.

### Phase 1: credibility

Proof, reassurance, trust, and access support.

### Phase 2: friction

Form path, portal flow, CTA clarity, and speed.

### Phase 3: scale

Retargeting, nurture, design refresh, and rollout.
