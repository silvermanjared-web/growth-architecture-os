# Governance

This repo uses governance to keep the public version useful, accurate, and source-aware.

## Review lanes

- Source support
- Claim language
- Voice consistency
- Public-safety review
- Structure review

## Claim standard

Use `00-positioning/proof-points.md` as the canonical claim bank and metric-control file.

Do not make a claim sound cleaner or more certain than the material behind it.

## Public-safety standard

The repo should show judgment without exposing confidential detail.

Avoid raw internal material, private employee details, unsupported metrics, and legal or HR-sensitive content.

## Structure standard

Public navigation should point to real files, not placeholders or internal work-in-progress notes.
