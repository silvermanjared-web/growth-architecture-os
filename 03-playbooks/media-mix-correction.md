# Media Mix Correction Playbook

## Purpose

Use this when the channel mix looks active but the business outcome is not moving enough.

## Core question

Is the current mix creating the right kind of demand, or only the easiest demand to count?

## Diagnostic steps

1. Compare volume against downstream movement.
2. Review each channel by its actual role.
3. Identify paths where quality is stronger than cost suggests.
4. Identify paths where cost looks efficient but quality is weak.
5. Check whether missing or delayed data is distorting the read.
6. Rebalance around business outcome, not platform comfort.

## Channel role reminders

- Search captures existing intent.
- Social can create demand, deliver proof, and support retargeting.
- Video can handle reassurance and consideration.
- Automated campaign types need clean signals.
- Retargeting helps recover people who did not convert the first time.

## Output

A useful correction plan should include:

- Current mix
- Proposed mix
- Business rationale
- Risks
- Guardrails
- Measurement needs
- Owner and timing

## Standard

The best mix is not the cheapest mix. It is the mix that produces the clearest path to the business outcome at a risk level leadership understands.
