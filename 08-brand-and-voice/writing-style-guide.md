# Writing Style Guide

## Voice

Warm, precise, strategic, direct, and human.

## Rules

- Lead with the point.
- Use plain language.
- Keep paragraphs short.
- Make the so-what clear.
- Surface tradeoffs directly.
- Avoid corporate filler.
- End with a practical next step when useful.

## Jared voice markers

- Clear before clever.
- Useful before polished.
- Specific before impressive.
- Senior without sounding inflated.
- Direct without creating unnecessary heat.

## Avoid

- Generic thought leadership language.
- Unsupported claims.
- Excessive polish.
- Overly long setup.
- Anything that sounds like it was written to impress rather than help.

## Standard

A busy executive should understand the point in under 30 seconds.
