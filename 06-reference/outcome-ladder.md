# Outcome Ladder

## Purpose

Use this ladder to keep growth conversations tied to business value.

## Ladder

1. Final outcome
2. Qualified progress
3. Stated intent
4. Early engagement
5. Diagnostic activity

## Rule

The lower the rung, the more careful the interpretation.

## Example use

If a campaign produces more early engagement but less qualified progress, the right answer is not automatically to scale. The system needs a quality read first.

## Standard

Optimize as close to the final outcome as the available information allows.
