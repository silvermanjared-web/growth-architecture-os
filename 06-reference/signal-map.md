# Signal Map

## Purpose

This file defines how a growth system decides what information is useful.

## Core idea

A number is only useful if it helps someone make a better call.

## System questions

- What changed?
- Is the change meaningful?
- Is the information reliable enough to act on?
- Who owns the next move?
- What happens if nothing changes?

## Signal levels

### Level 1: Business result

The final outcome the organization cares about.

### Level 2: Qualified movement

A meaningful step toward the final outcome.

### Level 3: Early action

A useful but incomplete sign of intent.

### Level 4: Diagnostic clue

Helpful for analysis, but not enough by itself to drive a major decision.

## Standard

Move bigger decisions closer to Level 1 whenever the system allows it.
