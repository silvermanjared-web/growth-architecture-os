# Decision Signal Notes

## Principle

A system is only useful when leaders know what they can trust.

## The work

Clarify:

- Which numbers matter
- Where they come from
- Who owns them
- How often they are reviewed
- What action they support

## Readout standard

A useful readout should show:

- What changed
- Why it matters
- What is uncertain
- What should happen next

## Standard

Do not make large moves from unclear information.
